About

Neurotech@UPenn is a collection of computer scientists and neuroscience researchers inspired by the human brain and working to replicate its core principles on the path to AGI.

We operate at the interface of brain circuits, neurotechnology, and modern AI.

Mission

UPenn Prestige exists to make Penn a hub for brain-inspired computation — where ideas flow between neuroscience and AI.

  • Build a rigorous, computation-first community.
  • Design models connecting cortical circuits to deep learning.
  • Create bridges between BCIs and AI.
  • Support student-led research pushing scalable AI.

Research Areas

We explore the intersection of neural circuits, attention, memory, RLHF, and scalable deep learning.

Brain-inspired deep learning Attention & transformers Reinforcement learning & RLHF Computational & systems neuroscience Neurotechnology & BCIs Alignment & safety primitives
