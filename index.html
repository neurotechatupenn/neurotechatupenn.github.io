About

UPenn Prestige is a student-led research identity highlighting innovation in Neurotechnology, Machine Learning, Cognitive Systems, and Computational Neuroscience at the University of Pennsylvania. The initiative focuses on credibility, outreach, and meaningful scientific engagement with top global institutions.

Mission

  • Raise the visibility and trust of UPenn Neurotech initiatives.
  • Engage with top CS and AI departments across the world.
  • Host high-quality conversations with leading researchers.
  • Showcase strong, upcoming research talent from Penn.
  • Bridge Neurotech, AI Safety, and Cognitive Systems research.

Research Areas

Neurotech Machine Learning Computational Neuroscience Robotics Cognitive Systems AI Safety LLMs & Reasoning Brain-Computer Interfaces Reinforcement Learning

Prestige Talks

Prestige Talks is a conversation-driven initiative featuring researchers in AI, neuroscience, computer science, robotics, cognitive systems, and computational theory. These discussions are intentionally short, high-signal, and designed to foster insightful academic exchange.
